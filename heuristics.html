Amazon.com

Issues that Tom found

1. Requires login to see languages or change them

2. Doesn’t tell you right away

3. Change language option doesn’t have many options

4. Famous website, a bit difficult to navigate

Issues that Brenda found

1. A lot to look, overwhelming, location is off

2. Misleading titles, new releases change to new item releases/inventory

3. Misleading language change/country

4. No chat box for reaching customer service or representative

5. Forcefully have to log in to chat/frustrating/gift

Issues that Tanner found

1. Eye catching pictures and description (This is a good thing).

2. Ads scattered

3. Place customer service option where it’s easier to find

4. You shouldn’t have to sign in for regular things/should be a continue as guest option or maybe just not as to sign in or continue as guest
